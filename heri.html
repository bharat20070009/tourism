Historic Landmarks

From ancient stupas to sacred temples, Amaravathi is home to remarkable sites that reflect its deep history and spiritual essence.

Amaravathi Stupa - An ancient Buddhist marvel with intricate carvings.

Amareswara Swamy Temple - A sacred pilgrimage site dedicated to Lord Shiva.

Dhyana Buddha - A towering meditative statue symbolizing peace.

Undavalli Caves - Rock-cut caves with a blend of Hindu and Buddhist influences.
